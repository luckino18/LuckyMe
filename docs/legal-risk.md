# Legal Risk

LuckyMe may be considered gambling, lottery, sweepstakes, promotional gaming, or
another regulated activity in some jurisdictions because it combines payment,
chance, prizes, and protocol fees.

No legal review has been completed. This repository must not be used for
real-money mainnet launch until qualified counsel signs off on the exact product,
jurisdictions, economics, app copy, terms, and operating model.

## DEVNET_STORE_DEMO

The current store-demo mode is devnet only:

- no real SOL
- no real-money prizes
- no claim of mainnet readiness
- visible app banner: `DEVNET MODE - no real funds`

## Mainnet Legal Checklist

- written jurisdiction review
- terms of service
- privacy policy
- responsible gaming policy
- age gate
- geofencing
- tax and payout policy
- sanctions/AML review if applicable
- sweepstakes/free-entry analysis if applicable
- customer support and dispute process
- store listing copy reviewed for non-misleading claims

Do not remove devnet warnings until this checklist is complete.
