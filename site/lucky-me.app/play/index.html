How to Play

Full guide

Buy tickets. Reach the target. Let the round finish.

Each ticket has an equal chance. Buying more tickets increases your chance in Mini, Normal, and High, within the pool limit.

  1. 1

    Choose a pool

    Mini, Normal, High, or Premium each has its own ticket price and target.

  2. 2

    Connect your wallet

    You stay in control. LuckyMe never asks for your seed phrase.

  3. 3

    Buy tickets

    Review the amount, then approve the purchase only inside your wallet.

  4. 4

    The clock starts

    The first confirmed ticket starts the one-hour round.

  5. 5

    Reach the ticket target

    Mini 25, Normal 13, High 3, and Premium 3 total tickets.

  6. 6

    Valid draw

    When the target is reached, the winner or winners are selected after the hour ends.

Automatic refund if the target is missed

If the minimum is not reached before the round ends, no winner is drawn. 100% of the ticket purchase amount is automatically returned to the wallet that bought the tickets.

No claim button is required. Solana network fees are not refundable.

Total tickets, not total players

Mini needs 25 tickets sold in total, not 25 different players. The same total-ticket rule applies to Normal and High. Premium is different: it allows one ticket per wallet and needs three distinct wallets for its three winners, split 70% / 20% / 10%.

Wallet

You hold the keys

LuckyMe uses self-custody wallet flows. It never asks for seed phrases or private keys, and the backend never signs player transactions.
