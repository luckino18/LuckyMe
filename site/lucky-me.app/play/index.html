LuckyMe guide

How to Play

Everything needed for the four public Solana pools.

Mainnet
Mini0.005 SOL25 tickets · 1 winner
Normal0.01 SOL13 tickets · 1 winner
High0.05 SOL3 tickets · 1 winner
Premium0.1 SOL3 wallets · 3 winners
  1. 1
    Choose and buy

    Connect your wallet, choose tickets, and approve only inside your wallet. One refundable rent deposit covers that wallet entry, regardless of how many tickets it buys at once.

  2. 2
    First ticket starts the timer

    The one-hour countdown begins only after the first confirmed ticket.

  3. 3
    Target reached

    After the timer, a valid round uses verifiable Solana randomness to select the winner or winners.

  4. 4
    Target missed

    No winner is drawn. 100% of the ticket purchase amount and entry rent return automatically; Solana network fees do not.

Total tickets, not total players

Mini, Normal, and High count tickets. Premium is different: one ticket per wallet, three distinct wallets, and three winners split 70% / 20% / 10%. No claim button is required. If another player wins a valid round, your rent deposit still returns automatically.

You hold the keys

LuckyMe uses self-custody wallet flows. It never asks for seed phrases or private keys, and the backend never signs player transactions.
