How to Play LuckyMe

Choose a pool, buy tickets, and help the round reach its total-ticket target. The first confirmed ticket starts a one-hour clock.

Equal chance per ticket 1 hour from first ticket Automatic refunds
Step by step

From wallet connection to result

  1. 1

    Choose a pool

    Pick Mini, Normal, High, or Premium. Each pool has a fixed ticket price and target.

  2. 2

    Connect your wallet

    You remain in control of your wallet. LuckyMe never asks for your seed phrase or private key.

  3. 3

    Buy tickets

    Every ticket has an equal chance. More tickets mean a greater chance, within the pool limit.

  4. 4

    One temporary rent deposit

    Your wallet pays one small refundable rent deposit for its entry in the round, regardless of how many tickets it buys at once. It returns automatically when the entry closes.

  5. 5

    The first ticket starts the clock

    The round lasts one hour from the first confirmed ticket, not from when the empty round appears.

  6. 6

    Reach the ticket target

    Mini needs 25 total tickets, Normal 13, High 3, and Premium 3.

  7. 7

    Valid draw

    If the target is reached, the winner or winners are selected after the one-hour round ends.

What comes back automatically

If the target is missed, no winner is drawn. Your ticket money and refundable rent deposit return automatically.

If the target is reached and another player wins, your ticket stays in the prize pool, but your rent deposit still returns automatically when your entry closes.

No claim button is required. Solana network fees are not refundable.

Important clarification

The target counts tickets, not players

Mini needs 25 tickets sold in total, not 25 different players. One wallet may buy all 25 Mini tickets in one entry. The same total-ticket rule applies to Normal and High.

Premium is the exception: it allows one ticket per wallet and needs three distinct wallets. Its three winners share the main prize 70% / 20% / 10%.
