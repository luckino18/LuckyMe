Service

LuckyMe is a Solana web and mobile application for fixed-entry pool rounds. Users connect a compatible Solana wallet, review transaction details, and decide whether to sign transactions in their own wallet.

Wallet Control

LuckyMe does not ask for seed phrases or private keys. The backend prepares unsigned transactions. Your wallet controls signing, approval, rejection, and fee payment.

Network And Transaction Risk

Solana transactions are irreversible once confirmed. Network fees, congestion, RPC availability, wallet behavior, and on-chain program state can affect transaction outcomes.

Availability

LuckyMe may show unavailable states when backend, RPC, wallet, or on-chain data cannot be verified. Production does not use fake pool data as a replacement for mainnet state.

LuckyMe Seeker Pass Promotion

Entry is free and requires one authentication signature from a wallet that holds an eligible LuckyMe Seeker Pass NFT. One wallet and one NFT asset may each create only one entry. No purchase or Solana transaction is required to enter.

The campaign closes automatically at 1,000 unique verified entries. Twenty unique winners are selected from the frozen ordered entry commitment using a previously committed future finalized Solana blockhash. The published prize schedule totals 3 SOL. Payouts remain disabled until prize funding is confirmed, and ownership may be rechecked before payment. The promotion is void where prohibited; entrants are responsible for complying with local law and providing any information reasonably required for prize delivery.
